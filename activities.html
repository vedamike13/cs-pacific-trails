Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjecent to a state park. Go it alone or join
one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching
trips at sunrise several times a week."
